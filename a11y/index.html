You are here: Home→ Accessibility

As an accessibility advocate, Justin understands the importance of digital accessibility. He aims to make this site accessible to as many people as possible, including compliance with WCAG 2.1 AA-level success criteria for all content on this site.

While Justin strives for accessibility, he is also human and knows mistakes happen. If you encounter any accessibility issues with this site, please contact Justin to let him know about the issues you are experiencing so they can be addressed.
