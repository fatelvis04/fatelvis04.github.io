About Justin

Justin Yarbrough is blind, a guide dog handler, and an accessibility specialist. He works for Rio Salado College, an online community college in the Phoenix area, focusing on the accessibility of online course content. Justin is the president of Ability Maricopa, an affinity resource group for employees with disabilities within the Maricopa County Community College District (MCCCD) and serves as a representative on the district’s employee council.

Justin also writes about blindness, service animal, and accessibility-related topics on a freelance basis and has appeared on podcasts and other media to discuss these topics. A sampling of Justin's writing and other media appearances can be found on the media page of this website. If you would be interested in having Justin write for you or appear on a video or podcast, you can contact him here.

In his spare time, Justin enjoys sports, traveling, technology, and spending time with his wife, cat, and two dogs.
