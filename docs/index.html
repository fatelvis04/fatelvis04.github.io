About Justin

Justin is a blind digital accessibility coach living in the Phoenix area. He writes about blindness, service animal, and accessibility-related topics on a freelance basis and has appeared on podcasts and other media to discuss these topics. A sampling of Justin's writing and other media appearances can be found on the writing and other media pages on this site. If you would like to have Justin write for you or appear on a podcast or other media, you can contact him.

In his spare time, Justin enjoys sports, traveling, technology, and spending time with his wife, dog, and two cats.

You can Follow Justin on Twitter.
