About Justin

Justin Yarbrough is blind, a guide dog handler, and an accessibility specialist. He works for Rio Salado College, an online community college in the Phoenix area, focusing primarily on the accessibility of online course content. Justin is currently the president of Ability Maricopa, an affinity resource group for employees with disabilities and allies withing the Maricopa County Community College District (MCCCD), in which Rio Salado College is a member institution and serves as a representative on the district’s employee council.

Outside of Rio and MCCCD, Justin also writes about blindness, service animal, and accessibility-related topics on a freelance basis and has appeared on podcasts and other media to discuss these topics.

In his spare time, Justin enjoys sports, traveling, technology, and spending time with his wife, cat, and two dogs.
