Media

Below you will find a collection of items in which Justin has shared his expertise. This page will be updated when new things are made available.

If you would like Justin to write for you or appear on a podcast or other media, please contact him here.

Nature

  • Using alt text to make science Twitter more accessible for people with visual impairments : Co-written with Dr. Domenico Chiarella and Dr. Christopher Jackson, this article examines how alt text can be used on science-based Twitter posts to make them more accessible to people with visual impairments.

Runway Girl Network

  • Why US animal relief areas are ripe for improvement: Discussing experiences with service animal relief areas in airports and how they could be improved.
  • Minor hiccups aside, United strives to be accessible for the blind: Trip report on a flight between Phoenix to Houston and back in late April 2021.
  • Exploring airline form accessibility for service animal handlers: Examining the accessibility of the forms used to submit service animal information soon after the new rules for flying with service animals were adopted. The issues that were identified with United and Alaska's forms have since been resolved.
  • DOT service animal rule lifts some proposed burdens on handlers: Article examining the new rules adopted by the DOT surrounding flying with service animals.
  • United impresses with accessible app for the blind: Article examining recent updates to United's iPhone app in which United touted all the accessibility improvements that were made.
  • Flying blind in a post-pandemic world – it’s complicated: Article speaking to blind people about their thoughts on traveling by air just as air travel was beginning to rebound during the COVID-19 pandemic.
  • Op-Ed: DOT NPRM could discourage service animal owners from traveling: Article reacting to the proposed rules for traveling with service animals that were issued by the US Department of Transportation while the proposal was open for comment.
  • Op-Ed: DOT made the right call with service animal guidance: Article discussing guidance provided by the US Department of Transportation on flying with service animals under the previous rules.
  • Op-Ed: Airlines should follow United lead on seatback IFE accessibility: Article discussing a newly-revealed seatback in-flight entertainment system United was beginning to install that has a plethora of accessibility features, including a built-in screen reader, screen magnification, and assistance for color blind passengers.

Other
